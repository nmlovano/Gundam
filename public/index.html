WELCOME TO MY LOVE OF GUNPLA!!!

My love for Gundam came in the early 2000's when Gundam Wing first aired on Toonami. Shortly after I found Gunpla, or Gundam model kits. That's when my love for Gunpla really began to take hold. When I first started building Gunpla, I was in my mid-teens. I obviously didn't have a lot of money at the time so I didn't have much of a collection back then. Shortly after high school, I joined the Marines. Unfortionately it was at this time that I lost all of my Gunpla. In 2016, I was looking for a new hobby and came across a store in Tampa that sold Gunpla while I was on vacation. I walked into a store at the mall and found a huge display of them. It was at this point that I decided I was going to get back into building them. When I returned from my trip, I searched Amazon and found a few entry level kits that I felt would be a good starting point to get back into them. I had no idea what I was getting into. That was in November of 2016. Since then my collection has grown to about 50 kits that range in all sizes.
